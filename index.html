Our Vision

The founders of Trek, as Project Manager and Software Engineer at Amazon, deeply understand the shortcomings of traditional education. We found that teachers and college counselors often overlook students' career prospects, leading many to realize their lack of interest in their chosen field only after entering university, ultimately facing unemployment upon graduation. To this end, we established Trek to help students establish their interests early and grow in their professional fields.
